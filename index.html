🙋 Become a Loady

Earn money from home by doing laundry for your neighbors. Work on your own schedule, set your preferences, and get paid per load.

❓ FAQ for Loadys

  • How do I get paid? You’ll receive direct deposits weekly based on completed loads.
  • What supplies do I need? Just your washer/dryer, detergent, and space to fold. LoadShare provides care guides.
  • How are customers assigned? You’ll be matched based on distance, availability, and preferences.
